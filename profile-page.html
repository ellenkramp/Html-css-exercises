Maynard James Keenan

Rock Legend

  • Born James Herbert Keenan; April 17, 1964
  • American musician, record producer, winemaker, and actor
  • Best known as the vocalist for Grammy Award-winning progressive metal band Tool
  • Originally from Ohio, but spent his high school and college years in Michigan
  • Served in the United States Army in the early 1980s
  • Attended Kendall College of Art and Design in Grand Rapids
